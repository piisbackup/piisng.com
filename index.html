Welcome @ PIIS Nigeria

PIIS Nigeria is an accredited partner of PII Solutions UK and currently boast over 25+ developer team and growing. We have over 15 years of experience in IT, creating cutting-edge complex B2B and B2C web systems and supporting Sage/SAP/ZOHO applications, as well as delivering top-notch direct outsourcing.

PIIS Nigeria office located in Awosika off Opebi Rd, Ikeja in the heart of Lagos is concentrated on delivering developer out-staffing, development of web solutions and partner support on Sage/SAP/ZOHO with high integrity. We do not always try to be the cheapest. However, thanks to our location you will be surprised of the reasonable prices we offer.

Our clients are worldwide. PIIS Nigeria works in consonance with PIIS offices around the world to handle onsite projects or background projects. Our global presence affords us the opportunity to offer solutions to our customers at a globalized scale, in terms of differences in time-zone, software language, currency, regional settings and local preferences.
